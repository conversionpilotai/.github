# ConversionPilot AI

**AI sales assistants for real estate websites.**

ConversionPilot AI helps real estate agencies turn website visitors into qualified leads using intelligent AI conversations, buyer qualification, and CRM-ready lead capture.

## What We Build

* AI website sales assistant
* Real estate lead qualification widget
* Qualified lead dashboard
* Website chat automation
* CRM-ready lead capture
* Real estate demo templates

## First Focus

We are starting with **Dubai real estate agencies**.

Our assistant qualifies property visitors by asking:

* Buy, rent, or invest?
* Preferred location
* Budget range
* Bedrooms
* Timeline
* Name
* Phone or WhatsApp
* Email

## Why It Matters

Most real estate websites lose serious buyers because visitors leave before speaking to an agent.

ConversionPilot AI helps agencies:

* Engage visitors 24/7
* Qualify serious buyers automatically
* Reduce wasted sales calls
* Capture high-intent leads
* Send qualified opportunities to the sales team
