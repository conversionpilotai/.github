# ConversionPilot AI

AI sales assistants for real estate websites.

ConversionPilot AI helps real estate agencies engage visitors, qualify property buyers, and capture high-intent leads directly from their websites.

## What We Build

- AI website sales assistant
- Real estate lead qualification widget
- Qualified lead dashboard
- Website chat automation
- CRM-ready lead capture
- Real estate demo templates

## First Focus

We are starting with Dubai real estate agencies.

Our assistant qualifies visitors by asking:

- Buy, rent, or invest?
- Preferred location
- Budget range
- Bedrooms
- Timeline
- Name
- Phone or WhatsApp
- Email

## Technology

- Next.js
- React
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase

- Vercel

## Mission

Help real estate businesses stop losing website visitors and turn more conversations into qualified sales opportunities.
